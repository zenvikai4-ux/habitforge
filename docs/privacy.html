Privacy Policy for HabitForge

Last updated: June 2026

HabitForge ("the App") is developed and published by Zenvik AI. This Privacy Policy explains how the App handles your information.

Summary

HabitForge is designed to work entirely offline. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to our servers or any third party. All your habits, reminders, names, and progress data stay on your device.

Information We Do Not Collect

HabitForge does not require account creation, sign-in, or any form of registration. We do not collect:

  • Your name, email address, or contact information
  • Your location
  • Your device identifiers for tracking or advertising purposes
  • Any habit data, notes, or personal entries you create in the app

Data Stored Locally on Your Device

The App stores the following information locally on your device only, using an on-device database (SQLite):

  • Habits you create, including names, schedules, and reminder times
  • Your completion history and streak data
  • Your chosen display name and avatar, if you set one during onboarding
  • App preferences and settings

This data is never transmitted off your device. If you uninstall the App, this data is permanently deleted.

Notifications

If you enable reminders, the App schedules local notifications on your device to remind you about your habits. These notifications are generated and delivered entirely on-device and do not involve any external server.

Advertising

HabitForge is free to use. To unlock optional features (such as adding more than 5 habits or unlocking a streak freeze), the App may show a short rewarded video advertisement. Advertising is served through standard mobile ad networks, which may collect limited technical information (such as device type or advertising identifiers) in accordance with their own privacy policies, for the purpose of serving and measuring ads. We do not control or have access to this data beyond what the ad network provides in aggregate, anonymized form.

Children's Privacy

HabitForge does not knowingly collect any personal information from anyone, including children. Since the App requires no account, sign-in, or personal data entry, no personal information is collected from any user.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties, because we do not collect or have access to any of your personal or habit data in the first place.

Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device and is never transmitted to us, the security of your data depends on the security of your own device. We recommend using your device's standard security features (such as a passcode or biometric lock) to protect your data.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. Continued use of the App after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
